We know many of you will be travelling to celebrate with us, which means the world! Here is some information to help you plan your trip.

Getting There

By Car

Both venues are easily accessible by car and have parking available for guests. Harrowden Hall will allow cars to be left overnight but they must be collected by 10:00 AM the following morning.

By Train

The nearest major station is Wellingborough, which is a short taxi ride from Harrowden Hall. We recommend pre-booking a taxi.

Local Taxis

  • Bounds Taxis: 01933 222222
  • Lee Cabs: 01933 442444

Your Questions Answered

When should I RSVP by?

Please let us know if you can make it by August 1st, 2026 by clicking the RSVP tab on our website.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. Thank you for your understanding!

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only celebration. We hope you enjoy having the evening off!

Do you have a gift registry?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all. However, should you wish to honour us with a gift, a contribution to our honeymoon fund would be much appreciated.

I have a dietary requirement, what should I do?

Please let us know about any dietary requirements or allergies when you RSVP so we can pass this information on to our caterers.
